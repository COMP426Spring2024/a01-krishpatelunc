The Legend of The Loch Ness Monster

Significant Sightings:

Year Description
565 St Columbia, a Christian Monk described a monster that he encountered on the lake.
1933 Mr and Mrs Spicer see the beast. Reviving old interests about the myth.
1934 Famous Surgeon Photo hoax released to public.
1955 A bank manager releases a doctored photo of a moving body in the water.
1972 Dr Robert Rines uses an under-water imaging device and records a flipper-like shape.
1972 Monster hunter Frank Searle takes images of what are later found to be tree trunks.
1987 Operation Deepscan, the largest and most scientific search of Loch Ness using sonar monitors, begins.
2003 The BBC sponsors a search of Loch Ness.
